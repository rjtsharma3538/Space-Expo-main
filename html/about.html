Space Tech Expo USA returns to Long Beach, CA, May 01*-03, 2022


Now approaching its 10th edition, Space Tech Expo USA will return to Long Beach, California this coming May.


Originally launched at the time of the final American Space Shuttle program mission landing on earth, Space Tech Expo USA quickly established itself as the major supply-chain meet as a new era of private industry commercial space ushered in. We invite you to join us this year in California, boasting the highest number of Aerospace Engineers of any state, where the space community will have the chance to connect and do business in the sunny space industry hub of LA.
Stay ahead of the curve on space technology, solutions and innovation at the leading showcase for space industry manufacturing & testing services, components and systems engineering for spacecraft, launcher and satellite programs this May at Space Tech Expo USA.








EXPERIENCE THE LATEST TECHNOLOGIES IN THE EXHIBITION HALL


Meet hundreds of suppliers and manufacturers from all sectors of the aerospace supply chain at Space Tech Expo USA, offering the opportunity to meet your competitors, peers and new contacts in an unmatched face-to-face setting. Our exhibitors will offer a showcase of the latest cutting-edge innovations, technologies and products that will help you secure your next manufacturing agenda.





ACCELERATING US SPACE SUPERIORITY AT THE CONFERENCE


The free-to-attend conference at Space Tech Expo provides forward-thinking, nationwide perspectives from industry experts that will expand your knowledge on key industry issues and opportunities at present. In 2022, the agenda will center around the theme ‘maintaining and growing a strategic, strong and inclusive US space supply chain’, and will highlight all aspects involved in sustaining a strong and leading nation in Earth’s orbits and beyond.




GENERATE A YEAR'S WORTH OF BUSINESS CONNECTIONS IN TWO DAYS


From industry-leading manufacturers and suppliers to systems integrators, contractors and buyers, Space Tech Expo USA welcomes the strongest calibre of aerospace community members each year. With unlimited networking opportunities available, including a bespoke B2B meetings service, we will put you in the right place with the right people to help meet your next business match.
